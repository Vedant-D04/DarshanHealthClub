OUR STORY

Building champions and healthy lifestyles since 1983.

Darshan Health Club was established in 1983 with a single goal: to provide the best fitness instruction in Mumbai. Founded by **Mr. Darshan Aurora**, a celebrated bodybuilder and IBFF National Silver medalist, the club has been a landmark in the fitness community for over 40 years.

Today, the club is personally supervised by Mr. India Darshan Aurora and **Physical Culturist Siddharth Aurora**, ensuring that every member receives expert guidance tailored to their physiological needs.

"We don't just provide equipment; we provide the knowledge and environment needed for real, lasting transformation."

DARSHAN AURORA

Founder & Director

1983-84 IBFF National Silver Medalist. Expert in physical culture and competitive bodybuilding training.

SIDDHARTH AURORA
